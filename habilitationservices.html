Habilitation Services

Support – Hourly

Zion provides habilitation and special developmental skills to our members help them to improve their daily living skill towards independent living. Zion offer these services in our member's home or in the community and might include activities to help the member learn to become independent. Service includes but not limited to; how to use the bathroom or to cook a meal or balance a check book. The services provided are specific to what the member may need to learn to become more independent or be a valued member of his/her community based on his/her own choices in certain areas. “We work with the abilities and the disabilities.”.

Individually Designed Living Arrangement

Zion is authorized to provide this services in non-licensed residential living situation for Division members. Our members choose where and with whom they will live and assume all responsibility for their residence. Generally, one (1) or more members reside together in a private residence that is leased or owned by the member(s) and/or the member(s) representative. The focus of this service is to provide habilitative supports to these members based on the collective need for direct staff support to eligible members who have chosen to reside together and share their resources.

Zion is committed to provide a broad array of support services to promote the physical, emotional, and mental well-being of our members. It is our goal to provide this services to enable each member to acquire knowledge and skills and be a value member of his/her community based on his/her own choices. Some of the objectives of this service is to provide training and supervision to each member to increase or maintain his/her self-help, socialization, and adaptive skills to live and participate in the community, and develop positive relationships for the members in their community.

Supported Developmental Home (Adult & Foster Care Child) - With Room & Board

Zion provide exceptional services to recruit, study, train, monitor, support, administrative supervision, and recommend families for licensing/re-licensing and/or certification of child and adult developmental homes. Zion provide this service to members who reside in licensed developmental homes (child or adult) that are subcontractors to Zion. Zion provide support to promote the physical, emotional, and mental well-being, to enable the member to acquire knowledge and skills and be a value member of his/her community based on his/her own choices.

Zion provides monthly consultation and support the provider to meet the needs of the member, transportation to support daily living activities, monthly progress reports on ISP outcomes and healthcare appointments, maintain monitoring reports, recruitment data/home studies, and inspection for each home. Zion will notify the Division immediately of any license that may be denied, suspended or revoke.

Group Home - Nursing Supported Group Home & DCS Receiving Home (Emergency Placement) - With Room & Board

Zion provides exceptional services to our members who have a variety of needs in a residential setting (24-hour day service, Room and Board), and a safe and healthy living environment. Zion develops pre-placement service and orientation system:

  • With ISP team to develop or revise the ISP within 30 days of admission/ change to a community residential settings or licensee
  • A specific teaching strategy for each functional outcome within thirty (30) days of admission to a community residential setting
  • Develop teaching plan or strategy for each objective assigned to the Group Home by the ISP team

    • Zion is committed to ensure each staff working in a community residential setting shall complete a pre-service orientation to specific need of the members. Zion has developed and revise its policies and procedures to address issues such as following which can occur in the operation of the setting: Incidents, Health related issues, Response to non-emergency conditions, Medical emergencies, Medical administration and storage, Instruction for documentations, Neglect and Abuse, etc

    Zion ensures that support services such as safe environment and transportation, are provided to promote the physical, emotional, and mental well-begin of the members. Zion staff are train to teach our members to acquire knowledge and skills that enable the members to participate in their community base on their choices.
