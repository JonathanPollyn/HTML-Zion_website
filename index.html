About us

Our Vision

Exceptional in services that improve the abilities of our members to obtain independence and quality of life

Our Mission

Zion Compassion Care strives to offer excellent home healthcare and community-based social and human services to individuals and families of Arizona State.

It is our goal to employ competent, compassionate, and well-trained individuals who are responsive to the needs of our consumers, families, and the communities we serve and to “work with the abilities not the disabilities of our members.”

Each staff member will meet the State of Arizona educational and training requirements for the services they provide. We encourage and support continued education of each service provider.

Zion Compassion Care is a Limited Liability Company based in Arizona, Owned by its principal investor. The initial office was established in Queen Creek, Arizona and now our main office is in Mesa, Arizona. Zion Compassion Care (Zion) was founded in 2009 and started operating on September 15, 2011
