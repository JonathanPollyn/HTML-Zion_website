About us

A Brief about Zion

Zion Started offering Home and Community Based Services (HCBS) and in 2014 extended its services to Group Homes. This year, 2019, Zion has opened her first Day Treatment Program Center for many types of day programs (DTA, DTT, DTS). We are excited to announce our Nursing Supported Group Homes as well. Zion offers a unique quality of health, social, and human services to qualify consumers in Arizona. We believe that with our competent, compassionate, well-educated staff and organized responsive leadership, we are the Home healthcare and community-based social and human services agency of choice in Arizona State.

Zion is founded on a Christian believes. However, we do not impose our Christian believes on anyone. Consumers of our services are individuals and families in need of community, home care, and group home services. Arizona Department of Economic Security/Division of Developmental Disability usually refer our consumers. Our Agency is contracted with Arizona Department of Economic Security/Division of Developmental Disability and licensed by Arizona Department of Health Services

Zion is registered with Arizona health care cost containment system (AHCCCS) and services reimbursed by Medicaid and other private insurance carriers. It is our policy to make sure we meet the requirements, regulations, guidelines and in compliance with department and division for providing home and community based, developmental home and group home services to consumers in Arizona State.

Zion Compassion Care is a Limited Liability Company based in Arizona, Owned by its principal investor. The initial office was established in Queen Creek, Arizona and now our main office is in Mesa, Arizona. Zion Compassion Care (Zion) was founded in 2009 and started operating on September 15, 2011..
